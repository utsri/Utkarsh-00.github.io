About

H e is presently working as Customer Advocate Automation Specialist at HPE and currently based out of India. He is responsible for driving multiple automation initiatives within the organisation. He plays a pivotal role in conducting periodic business reviews with customers by defining and tracking the outcome-oriented KPIs and in the planning of the contract renewals and business up-sells. Prior to this, he also worked as Senior Consultant at Cisco Systems.

He defines his life motive as, “Karmev Jayate”, which translates into the colloquial version as, ‘Work will always be Victorious’. Utkarsh’s corpus of work extends from the incorporation of Machine learning capabilities in different sectors to Blockchain Evangelism. He also works as a mentor for different developers as well as business communities, and along with all of this, he makes it a point to not miss his social contributions through various outreach initiatives.

But what really interests Utkarsh, is the chance to interact with passionate minds from all walks of life, and the opportunity to influence them. As a result, he has delivered various tech-talks on BigData, Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain across the globe. He has a vision to develop an intelligence, which will change the way we look at the world or in short to make 'Person Of Interest'.
